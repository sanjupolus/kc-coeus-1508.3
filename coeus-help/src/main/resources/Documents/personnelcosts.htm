Personnel Costs

The Personnel Costs navigation button expands to house the Project Personnel and Assign Personnel to Periods buttons.

The Project Personnel list can be thought of as a ‘team roster’ of persons that may work on the project – some may play in all periods, some may start in a later period, and some can just sit on the bench. Once on the list, you will select and Assign Personnel to Periods – e.g. add these persons to the budget in a personnel line item and determine their input by defining the duration (start & end date) and service (percentage of effort relationship to percentage charged) .  KC will inflate the salaries for annual salary increases, using the salary effective date, and then calculate direct and indirect costs based on the dates of performance duration and percentage of effort.
