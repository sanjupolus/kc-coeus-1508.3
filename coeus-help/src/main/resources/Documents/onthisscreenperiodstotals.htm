On this screen – Periods & Totals:

(A period row in Edit mode)

A.  Periods & Totals Left Navigation button: click to open the Periods & Totals screen.

B.  Recalculate and Reset buttons

o  Recalculate with Changes: calculates all budget periods.

o  Reset to period defaults: restores 12 month budget period rows.

C.   +Add Budget Period: use to enter another budget period within the existing start and end dates.

D.  Period Row Action buttons: a row for each period remains editable until a period detail budget is created

o  Edit: click to edit the fields to manually create a Summary Budget.; Converts to a Save button after use.

o  Trashcan icon: click to delete the project period row.

E.   Proposal Footer actions:

o  Continue: click to navigate to the next screen listed below on the left navigation bar

o  Save: click to save the proposal data and stay on this screen

o  Complete Budget: click to finalize the budget.
