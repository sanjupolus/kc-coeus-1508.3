You are here:KC Budget 6.0 > Budget Notes

On this screen –Budget Notes:

A.  Budget Notes: Left Navigation button click to open the Budget Notes screen.

B.  User ID and timestamp: Displays the user id and timestamp of the last update.

C.   Notes text box: Blank by default, but can be populated with information added in the budget, and then be edited, or can be used as a notepad by the user.

D.  Consolidate Expense Justifications Button: click to populate the Notes text field with data from the proposal.

E.   Proposal Footer actions:

o  Back: click to navigate to the previous screen on the left navigation bar

o  Save: click to save the proposal data and stay on this screen

o  Save and Continue: click to navigate to the next screen listed below on the left navigation bar

o  Complete Budget: click to mark the budget status as complete in the Budget Settings.

Note: request made to edit button label on this screen to “Consolidate Budget Notes” and to make the labels for the fields in the Personnel and Non-Personnel consistently be “Budget Notes.”
