On this screen – Project Income:

Ø Project Income: Use the left navigation button to open the screen.

Ø View Summary button: Click to open the window view to review the amount added to the table for each period, and a total of all income.

Ø Add Income: Click to add a period row to the table.

Ø Budget Period Detail row: Once added, the Budget Period, Description, and Amount can be edited in this row.

Ø Actions: Delete the row by clicking the trashcan icon.

Ø Proposal Footer actions:

o  Back: click to navigate to the previous screen on the left navigation bar

o  Save: click to save the proposal data and stay on this screen

o  Save and Continue: click to navigate to the next screen listed below on the left navigation bar

o  Complete Budget: click to mark the budget status as complete in the Budget Settings.
