You are here:KC Budget 6.0 > Project Personnel

On this screen – Project Personnel:

A.  Personnel Costs Left Navigation button: header for Project Personnel and Assign Personnel to Periods.

o  Click the right-facing triangle to expand and reveal the subcategory navigation options.

o  Click the downward-facing triangle to compact the subcategory navigation buttons.

B.  Project Personnel Left Navigation button. Click to open the screen to add and edit budget personnel.

C.   +Add Personnel and Sync from Proposal buttons

o  +Add Personnel: click to open the search window to add budget personnel.

o  Sync from Proposal: click to refresh the budget person list with all Key Personnel in the Proposal.

D.  From Proposal Development: Section header below which lists the persons sync’d from the Detail > Key Personnel screen.

E.   Other Personnel: Section header below which lists the persons added to the budget personnel list.

F.   Action buttons:

o  Details: click to open the edit person details window (e.g. base salary, appointment type, etc.)

o  Trashcan icon: click to delete the person from the budget personnel list.

G.  Proposal Footer actions:

o  Back: click to navigate to the previous screen on the left navigation bar

o  Save: click to save the proposal data and stay on this screen

o  Save and Continue: click to navigate to the next screen listed below on the left navigation bar

o  Complete Budget: click to finalize the budget.
